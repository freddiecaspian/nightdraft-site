Confirmed, not guessed

Most AI tools try to learn your restaurant by reading your old emails and hoping they got it right. NightDraft does the opposite. Your facts are written down once, checked by you, and locked.

Confirmed by you

Your prices, opening hours, party-size rules, cancellation policy and corkage are written down once and signed off by you.

Read before every draft

NightDraft reads your rulebook before it writes a single word, so the facts are never improvised on the spot.

Never invented

It will not make up a number or a policy. Anything that has not been confirmed with you comes back to you.

Your voice. Not a robot.

NightDraft learns how you write from your best, most careful replies, not the rushed two-liners you fire off mid-service. The voice can flex from one guest to the next. The facts underneath never move.

A safety net on every draft

Behind the writing sits a checker that reads every draft before it reaches you and holds back anything that is off, so a wrong reply never lands in your hands.

Every draft is checked

The menu has to match the price, the wording has to follow your house style, and the guest has to be greeted by name. If anything fails, the draft is held back.

Allergies are always flagged

If a guest mentions a serious allergy, NightDraft will not let a reply through that ignores it. It flags it for you and leaves it in your hands.

It physically cannot send

NightDraft only writes drafts. It has no send button. Every reply waits in your Gmail Drafts for your eyes and your send.
